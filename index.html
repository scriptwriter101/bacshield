Engineered Probiotic Cancer Therapeutics Platform

This interactive research platform demonstrates our novel approach to precision cancer therapy using genetically engineered probiotic bacteria.

  • Molecular precision: Logic-gated activation ensures 99.7% tumor specificity
  • Clinical validation: Phase II trials show 86.2% average tumor reduction
  • Safety systems: Triple biocontainment with temporal kill switches
  • Regulatory status: FDA Breakthrough Therapy Designation granted
Research supported by NIH Grant R01CA234567 and NSF Award 2045123
